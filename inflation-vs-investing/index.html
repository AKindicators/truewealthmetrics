Inflation vs Investing: Protecting Your Real Wealth

Understand how inflation affects savings and why long‑term investing helps preserve purchasing power.

Why inflation matters

Inflation reduces purchasing power over time. If your savings earn less than inflation, your real wealth declines even if the account balance rises.

Savings vs investing

Short‑term goals belong in cash; long‑term goals benefit from diversified investments that historically outpace inflation over decades.

Real returns

Focus on real return = investment return minus inflation. Fees also reduce real return, so keep costs low.

Practical strategy

Maintain an emergency fund in cash, then invest surplus using low‑cost funds and regular contributions.
