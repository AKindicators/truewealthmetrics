How to Budget for Beginners Step by Step

If you are new to budgeting, follow a simple step-by-step process:

  1. Calculate your total monthly income
  2. Track your real expenses for at least one month
  3. Separate essentials from non-essentials
  4. Set a savings goal before spending
  5. Adjust weekly to stay on track

This beginner budget plan ensures your money has a clear purpose.

Simple Budgeting Plan That Works

A simple budgeting plan is easier to stick with long term. Avoid overcomplicating your system.

  • Essentials (rent, utilities, groceries)

How to Save Money on Low Income

If you have a limited income, focus on small consistent improvements.

  • Reduce unnecessary spending
  • Automate small savings

Beginner Budget Example

Example: If you earn £2,000 per month, you might allocate:

  • £1,200 for essentials
  • £300 for savings
  • £200 for debt
  • £300 for flexible spending
