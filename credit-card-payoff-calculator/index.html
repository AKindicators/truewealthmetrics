How this calculator works

We apply your APR as a monthly rate, add interest each month, then subtract your payment (minimum + extra). The schedule is simulated until the balance reaches £0. Results are estimates and may differ from your lender’s calculations.

Quick tips
  • Even a small extra payment can save significant interest over time.
  • If you’re not making progress, increase your payment or look for a lower APR option.
  • Use the debt strategy tool to plan multiple debts together.
Why can a low payment mean you never pay off the balance?
If your payment is less than (or very close to) the monthly interest, the balance will not reduce. Increase payment or lower APR.
How does extra payment reduce interest?
Extra payment reduces principal faster, which lowers future interest charges and shortens payoff time.
Does this include fees or promotional APR periods?
No. This tool assumes a constant APR and fixed monthly payments. Fees, promos, and changing rates can change results.
